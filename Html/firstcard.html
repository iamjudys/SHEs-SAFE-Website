Do you believe that it was an insult for girls?

"Run Like a Girl" is a phrase that has historically been used as an insult, implying weakness or inferiority.

A famous example is the 2014 Always campaign titled "Like a Girl", which challenged gender stereotypes by showing how young girls see running,

throwing, or fighting "like a girl" as something strong and capable, while older individuals often mimic it as weak or silly.

The campaign encouraged people to rethink the phrase and celebrate female strength in all aspects of life.
